Visit Uluwatu

Experience world-class waves, breathtaking sunsets, and sacred cliffs in Bali's stunning southern coast.

Top three things to do in Uluwatu

Go out surfing, and enjoying the clear blue water

You can go to Dream beach, Bingin Beach, or check out some of the other beaches. They are all great for surfing.

Explore all of the famous cliffs around Uluwatu

You can go to Uluwatu Cliff, Karang Boma Cliff, or Nyang Nyang Cliff. The sunsets are also beautiful at the cliffs.

Relax, and enjoy all of the beautiful sunsets

A lot of spots are very good for watching the sunsets. Especially from a lot of restaurants, and cliffs.
